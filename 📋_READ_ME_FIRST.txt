📊 WHAT THE PLATFORM DOES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

INPUT:
  • Financial statements (CSV/Excel)
  • Investment memos (PDF/Word/TXT)
  • Company documents

PROCESSING (AI-Powered):
  • Extract financial data
  • Parse unstructured documents
  • Run 3 valuation methods (DCF, Comps, Precedent)
  • Identify risks and inconsistencies
  • Generate recommendations

OUTPUT:
  • Interactive dashboard with charts
  • Complete valuation analysis
  • Risk assessment (categorized)
  • Downloadable professional report
  • Investment recommendations
